HOD's message:

The Masters of Computer Application (MCA) is a two years full time postgraduate programme, sanctioned by AICTE, New Delhi and Govt. of Maharashtra with an intake capacity of 120. The programme is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and ours is the first institute to offer the programme under Faculty of Engineering. Department has attained its distinct identity since 2007. K. K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education and Research, Nashik has been granted Autonomy by UGC from the academic year 2022-2023.
