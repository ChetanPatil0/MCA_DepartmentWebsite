HOD Desk

Message from HOD's desk

The Masters of Computer Application (MCA) is a two years full time postgraduate programme, sanctioned by AICTE, New Delhi and Govt. of Maharashtra with an intake capacity of 120. The programme is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and ours is the first institute to offer the programme under Faculty of Engineering. Department has attained its distinct identity since 2007. K. K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education and Research, Nashik has been granted Autonomy by UGC from the academic year 2022-2023. The infrastructure includes well developed laboratories, adequate number of computers with latest configurations and internet facilities, spacious seminar hall with audio visual aids and class rooms as well as tutorial room. To enrich the quality and standard of teaching and learning through modern technology and science various facilities such as smart board, Data Projector, Overhead Projectors, License Softwares and Video Conferencing unit are available with the department. The department has received grants from the AICTE New Delhi for the MODROBs of laboratory in the year 2012-13 and workshop from BCUD, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune.  Department has Secured 4th rank in top ten promising MCA institutes in India, published in “HER Magazine”– July 2017 issue. The department has a well-qualified, experienced and enthusiastic faculties and technical staff. The faculty members play dual roles both as teacher to impart technical knowledge to students as well as a counsellor to guide them for their overall development. Department actively seeks to create opportunities outside of our curriculum for students to develop their professional skills through various certification programmes. Central library as well as department library is also available with the department. The ABACUS club, association of students is vibrant in organizing various events such as expert lectures, seminars, workshops, project competition and industrial visits on a regular basis. The result of the final year batch for the last fourteen (14) years is above 95%. This could not have been possible without wholehearted support received from all the stakeholders. Many of our students are rank holders at university level and are placed at multinational organizations with good salary packages and secured important positions. 


Dr. Vandana C. Bagal,
I/c Head of Department & Associate Professor
Dept. of MCA
